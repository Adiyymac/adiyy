Welcome to Our College

mlaharu uloom arabic college

The institute carries forward the old Ponnani tradition of scholar training.

Malharul Uloom Arabic College, located in Palamthodu, is known for its focus on Islamic and Arabic studies, blending traditional Islamic scholarship with modern educational approaches. The college emphasizes deep study in Arabic language and literature, Islamic jurisprudence, and related subjects, providing students with a solid foundation in both religious and secular knowledge. The institution attracts students from various parts of Kerala and beyond, creating a diverse learning environment. Programs at Malharul Uloom typically combine classroom-based learning with practical activities, such as seminars, workshops, and study circles
